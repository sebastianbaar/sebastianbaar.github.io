Memories



Memories makes it easy to turn your photos and videos into one single, continuous video — as a visual diary or as a freely curated project.

Two project types

1) Calendar Project (Year / Day)

Save one or multiple clips/photos per calendar year and per day. Perfect for “one moment a day,” travel, baby years, training, building projects, or creative challenges.

2) Free Project (no date)

Collect any media without a date reference and export it as a video — ideal for highlights, events, aftermovies, or social montages.

Quick media selection

  • Preselect directly from the iOS image browser / photo library
  • Photos are automatically converted into video segments (ready for export)

Export & customization

When exporting, you can personalize your video:

  • Set a cover image
  • Add music/audio
  • Show date text labels (for calendar projects)
  • Add multiple clips per day — everything becomes one large, seamless video

Who is Memories for?

  • Anyone who wants to keep a video diary
  • Creators who want to collect clips and export quickly
  • Families, travelers, projects, yearly recaps
